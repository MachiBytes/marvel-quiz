ABOUT US
This website is a group project created by Group 7, a team of five passionate Computer Science students, developed as part of our Web Development course. Our goal was to build something fun, interactive, and a little different from the usual quiz experience. We designed a unique Marvel-themed personality quiz that combines casual gameplay with a creative scoring system. By answering a series of questions, you’ll discover which Marvel character you’re most similar to — whether you’re a hero, a villain, or somewhere in between!
